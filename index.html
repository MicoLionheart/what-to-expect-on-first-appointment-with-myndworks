Preparing for Your First Appointment

Your first appointment at MyndWorks Psychiatry is an important step toward improving your mental health. The team is committed to making the process as smooth and stress-free as possible. Here’s what you need to know before your visit:

  • Complete the Intake Forms: Before your appointment, you’ll be asked to fill out intake forms to provide important information about your medical history and current concerns. You can learn more about their process on the About Us Page.
  • Bring Necessary Documents: Be sure to bring your ID, insurance information, and a list of any medications you’re currently taking.
  • Arrive Early: Arriving 10–15 minutes early ensures you have time to check in and complete any additional paperwork.

What Happens During the Appointment?

At your first appointment, you’ll meet with a compassionate and experienced provider who will take the time to understand your unique needs. Here’s what you can expect:

  1. Initial Assessment: Your provider will ask about your medical history, current symptoms, and goals for treatment.
  2. Discussion of Treatment Options: Based on your assessment, your provider will discuss potential treatment options, which may include therapy, medication management, or holistic approaches.
  3. Collaborative Planning: Together, you’ll create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs.

Learn more about their services on the Our Services Page.
